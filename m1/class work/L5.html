Facts about dog

Dogs, domesticated descendants of wolves, are popular pets known for their loyalty, intelligence, and diverse breeds, each with unique characteristics and temperaments, making them beloved companions for humans worldwide.

Dogs are a subspecies of the gray wolf (Canis lupus familiaris) and have been domesticated for thousands of years, evolving from wolves through selective breeding by humans.
